// The Process

How It Works

01

Create Account

Sign up and set up your profile. Browse available programs tailored to your career level — from fresh graduates to senior consultants.

02

Enroll & Pay

Pay securely via Paystack. Gain immediate access to program materials, session schedules, and the MRA Africa community.

03

Train & Practice

Attend intensive sessions with seasoned facilitators. Work through real case studies, hands-on practicals, and interactive classroom exercises.

04

Network & Grow

Exchange ideas with peers and faculty. Join the wider MRA Africa community and access mentorship, resources, and career opportunities beyond the program.

05

Get Certified

Receive your MRA Africa certificate of completion. Every graduate leaves with superior, noticeable skills and a credential recognised by the industry.

06

Launch Your Career

Share your certificate with employers and clients. MRA graduates have gone on to land roles at firms like IPSOS and lead research projects across Africa.
